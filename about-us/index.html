About Us

South Barn Consulting Ltd was set up in 2003 by its Director David Bailes BSc. CBiol. MSB FRQA.

David has experience in the food, chemical, and pharmaceutical industries in both sponsor and contract research organisations, and before setting up South Barn Consulting Ltd he was responsible for clinical quality assurance, quality management, and process improvement for a leading global pharmaceutical company.

Since 2003 South Barn Consulting Ltd has helped over 25 organisations to manage or improve their research quality. This has involved working at a strategic level, Research Quality Assurance (RQA) auditing, regulatory inspection readiness preparations, supplying training, and other service provision in many European countries, the US and South Africa.

To find out how South Barn Consulting Ltd can help you, or for more information please contact David through Contact Us.
